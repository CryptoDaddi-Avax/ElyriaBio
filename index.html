Frequently asked

Answers, not adjectives.

Are Elyria Bio peptides safe for human use?

No. All Elyria Bio products are sold strictly as in-vitro laboratory research materials for qualified research professionals and institutions. They are not for human or veterinary use, are not drugs, foods, or supplements, and have not been evaluated or approved by the FDA for the diagnosis, treatment, cure, or prevention of any disease.

What purity are Elyria Bio research peptides?

Every lot is held to a ≥99% reversed-phase HPLC purity floor before release; lots below the floor are rejected. Mean batch purity across the catalog is 99.3%. Molecular identity is confirmed by mass spectrometry against the expected monoisotopic mass.

Does every order include a certificate of analysis (COA)?

Yes. Each lot ships with a third-party certificate of analysis produced by an independent US laboratory — not in-house — reporting HPLC purity and mass-spec identity. Every vial's printed lot number resolves to its own COA and assay date.

How are the peptides shipped and stored?

Compounds are freeze-dried (lyophilized) and stored at −20°C to preserve integrity from synthesis through dispatch. Orders ship within 48 hours in protective, tamper-evident packaging. Free shipping applies to orders over $150. We ship to U.S. addresses only — international orders are not accepted.

Which research peptides does Elyria Bio sell?

The catalog spans 29 research peptides plus bacteriostatic water — among them GLP-2, GLP-3, BPC-157, TB-500, GHK-Cu, Ipamorelin, CJC-1295/Ipamorelin, Tesamorelin, NAD+, Epithalon, Semax, Selank, Melanotan I and II, DSIP, Kisspeptin, KPV, MOTS-c, PT-141, AOD-9604, Cagrilintide, IGF-1 LR3, 5-Amino-1MQ, Thymosin Alpha-1, SNAP-8, Glutathione, and the GLOW, KLOW and BPC-157/TB-500 blends — organized into Repair, Metabolic, Longevity, Growth, and Supplies categories. Prices range from $13 to $179 per vial.

Can I open a lab or institutional account?

Yes. Elyria Bio offers volume pricing, consolidated COAs, and net terms for verified institutions and qualified research professionals.
