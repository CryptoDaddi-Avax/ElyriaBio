How much does the affiliate program pay?

You earn 20% commission on each referred customer’s first order and 10% on all of their subsequent reorders, calculated on the order subtotal. There’s no cap on how much you can earn.

Referrals use a 60-day attribution window from the first click of your link. Once a customer signs up through your link, their future reorders keep earning you 10% for the life of the account.
