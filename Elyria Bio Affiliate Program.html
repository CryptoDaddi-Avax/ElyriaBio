Questions

Affiliate FAQ

Who can join the program?
Researchers, educators, science communicators, lab communities, and institutions whose audiences work with research materials. Because Elyria Bio products are strictly research-use-only, we review every application to ensure the audience and messaging are appropriate.
How and when do I get paid?
Commission accrues on delivered orders. You can request a payout once your balance clears the $50 minimum, or let automatic monthly batches handle it. We pay via PayPal, bank ACH, USDC, or store credit — your choice.
How does attribution work?
When someone clicks your referral link, we set a 30-day cookie. Any order they place within that window is credited to you. Customers can also enter your code at checkout, which attributes the order even without the cookie.
How do the commission tiers work?
You start at 10%. Once your lifetime referred revenue crosses $4,000 you move to 15% (Pro), and at $12,000 you reach 20% (Elite). Tier upgrades apply automatically to future orders — there's nothing to renegotiate.
What can I say about the products?
All Elyria Bio products are sold for in-vitro laboratory research only and are not for human or veterinary use. Affiliates must present them as research materials and must not make therapeutic, medical, or dosing claims. Approved swipe copy is provided in your portal to keep things compliant. See the full content & compliance guidelines.
Is there a cost to join?
No. The program is free to join and there are no minimums or quotas. You only need an approved application and an audience that fits the research-use-only context.
