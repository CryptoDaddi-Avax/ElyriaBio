Privacy policy

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, email, organization, shipping address, and order details — and limited technical information automatically, such as your browser type, device information, and pages viewed, to operate and improve the site. We do not seek to collect sensitive personal information, and we ask that you not send it to us.

2. Cookies & local storage

This site uses your browser’s local storage to remember your cart, recently viewed materials, your Research-Use acknowledgment, and display preferences. This data stays in your browser and is not, by itself, transmitted to us. You can clear it any time through your browser settings. We use only essential, first-party storage of this kind and do not use it for cross-site advertising.

3. How we use information

  • To process and ship orders and provide documentation
  • To verify eligibility and prevent prohibited or fraudulent use
  • To respond to inquiries and support requests
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the site
  • To comply with legal, tax, and regulatory obligations

4. Sharing

We share information only with service providers who help us operate — such as payment processors, shipping carriers, and IT vendors — under contracts that limit their use of it, and when required by law or to protect our rights. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5. Payment information

Payments are processed by third-party, PCI-DSS-compliant payment processors. Your full payment-card number is transmitted directly to the processor; we do not store complete card numbers on our systems. The processor’s handling of your payment data is governed by its own privacy policy.

6. Analytics & third-party services

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate site usage and improve performance. Where used, these services process limited technical data on our behalf. We do not permit them to use your information for their own advertising purposes.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a breach affecting your information, we will notify you and the authorities as required by law.

8. Your choices & rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and may opt out of non-essential communications, subject to legal limits, by contacting us. You can also manage cookies and local storage through your browser. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

9. California privacy rights

If you are a California resident, the CCPA/CPRA gives you the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, to request access to or deletion of it, to request correction, and to opt out of any “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law. To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below; we will verify your request and respond within the time required by law. You may use an authorized agent to submit a request.

10. Children’s privacy

This site is intended only for qualified researchers who are at least 21 years of age. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Do Not Track

Because there is no common industry standard for responding to browser “Do Not Track” signals, we do not currently respond to them. We do not track you across third-party websites for advertising regardless of any such signal.

12. Retention

We retain order and documentation records as needed to fulfill the purposes described here and to meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, after which we delete or de-identify them. Elyria Bio operates in the United States, and information is processed and stored there.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date above reflects the current version, and material changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact

Questions about this policy or to exercise your rights: privacy@elyriabio.com or via our contact page.
